# Wind Turbine Configurator – Crisis Response Playbook ⚠️🌬️

## 🎯 Purpose
Provide a structured framework for responding to **critical incidents** (system failures, compliance breaches, integration breakdowns) in the Wind Turbine Configurator software, ensuring **rapid recovery**, **stakeholder confidence**, and **minimal business disruption**.

---

## 🚨 Crisis Categories
- **SYS (System Outage)** → Configurator unavailable or degraded.  
- **SIM (Simulation Failure)** → Aerodynamic or load simulations produce invalid results.  
- **INT (Integration Breakdown)** → ERP/SCADA connectors fail or data mismatches occur.  
- **REG (Regulatory Non‑Compliance)** → IEC 61400 or local energy authority checks fail.  
- **SEC (Security Incident)** → Unauthorized access, data breach, or cyberattack.  

---

## 🛠️ Response Framework
1. **DET (Detection)** → Automated monitoring alerts or stakeholder reports.  
2. **COM (Communication)** → Notify crisis team, stakeholders, and OEM partners.  
3. **CON (Containment)** → Isolate faulty modules, disable unstable connectors.  
4. **RES (Resolution)** → Apply hotfix, rollback, or patch validated in staging.  
5. **VAL (Validation)** → Run smoke tests, compliance checks, and UAT sign‑off.  
6. **REP (Reporting)** → Document incident, root cause, and corrective actions.  

---

## 📊 Crisis Metrics
- **MTTD (Mean Time to Detect)** → Average time to identify incident.  
- **MTTR (Mean Time to Resolve)** → Average time to restore service.  
- **IMPACT (Business Impact Score)** → Severity rating based on downtime + stakeholder effect.  
- **SAT (Stakeholder Satisfaction)** → Feedback from OEMs, regulators, and field engineers.  

---

## 🔄 Continuous Improvement
- Conduct **Post‑Mortems** → Capture lessons learned.  
- Update **Playbook** → Refine detection, containment, and communication steps.  
- Share insights across teams → Build organizational resilience.  

---

## 🚦 Decision Gates
- **Go** → Crisis contained, validated, and stakeholders aligned.  
- **No‑Go** → Unresolved risks, compliance gaps, or failed validation.
