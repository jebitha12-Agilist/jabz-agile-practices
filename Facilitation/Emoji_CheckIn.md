# Icebreaker: Emoji Check‑In 🎭

## 🎯 Purpose
A quick 10‑second activity to instantly gauge team energy levels and create psychological safety before starting a session.

---

## 📝 How It Works
- **Two Choices** → Participants select one emoji to represent their current state:
  - **Calm** → Feeling steady, focused, ready to engage.  
  - **Pressure** → Carrying weight from deadlines, challenges, or stress.  
- **Prompt** → Ask: *“How are you feeling right now?”*  
- **Action** → Everyone drops their emoji on the Zoom Whiteboard or in chat.  
- **Timer** → Set a visible 10‑second countdown to keep it snappy.

---

## 🕒 Setting Up the Timer
- Display a countdown using your conferencing tool or a simple animated progress bar.  
- Share your screen with the timer slide.  
- Announce: *“Go!”* — the clock starts immediately.

---

## 🤝 Creating a Safe Space
- **Optional Sharing** → Participants can say “Pass” or stay silent.  
- **Time Check** → Keep shares brief (5–10 minutes total).  
- **Flow** → Pressure group shares first (30‑second snapshots), then Calm group shares grounding practices.  
- **Ground Rules** → One voice at a time, listen without interrupting, respect boundaries, honor silence.
