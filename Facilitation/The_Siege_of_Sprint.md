# The Siege of Sprint 🏰
*A Royal Retrospective*

## 🎯 Purpose
Recount the sprint as a medieval quest — celebrating victories, confronting dragons (challenges), and planning magical enhancements (improvements).

---

## ⚔️ Rules of the Realm
- **Prime Directive** → Everyone did their best given what they knew.  
- **Show Thy Face** → Cameras ON.  
- **Silence the Armour** → Mics OFF when others speak.  
- **Noble Speech** → Attack problems, never people.  
- **Seal of Secrecy** → What’s spoken in the castle stays in the castle.  
- **Unity of Command** → One voice at a time.

---

## 🧊 Icebreaker – Choose Your Class
- Knight → Defender of the realm.  
- Wizard → Master of arcane arts.  
- Blacksmith → Forging the future.  
**Activity:** Introduce yourself as a medieval profession for 30 seconds.

---

## 🏰 Glorious Victories
- **Iron Wall** → Stability and technical wins.  
- **Merchant’s Guild** → Value delivered to stakeholders.  
**Activity:** Share successes and give a “Knight’s Salute” 👏.

---

## 🐉 Fierce Dragons
- **Hidden Serpent** → Technical debt and hidden bugs.  
- **Fire‑Breather** → Urgent pressure and high‑stress tasks.  
- **Stone Giant** → Bottlenecks and bureaucracy.  
**Activity:** Identify beasts, discuss root causes, track them to their lair.

---

## 🔮 Magical Enhancements
- **Fortification Spells** → Strengthen current processes.  
- **Transmutation Spells** → Change how we work entirely.  
- **Elixirs of Speed** → Automation and velocity boosters.  
**Activity:** Vote with Mana Points 💎 on which spells to brew first.

---

## 🏆 Distributing the Spoils
- **Dragon Slayer** → Best problem‑solver.  
- **Royal Architect** → Best process improvement idea.  
- **Knight’s Shield** → Peer shout‑outs for support.  
**Activity:** Recognize contributions.

---
