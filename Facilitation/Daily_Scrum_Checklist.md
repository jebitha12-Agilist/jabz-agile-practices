# Daily Scrum Checklist ✅

## 🎯 Purpose
Provide a structured format for the **15‑minute Daily Scrum** to ensure transparency, alignment, and quick impediment resolution.

---

## 📝 Standard Questions
1. **Biggest Win (Yesterday)**  
   - What did you accomplish since the last Daily Scrum?  

2. **Biggest Risk (Blockers)**  
   - Any impediments preventing progress?  
   - Raise blockers early to resolve within 24 hours.  

3. **Learning (Insights)**  
   - What did you learn from yesterday’s work?  
   - Share knowledge to reduce silos.  

4. **Next Target (Today)**  
   - What is your top priority for the next 24 hours?  
   - Align with sprint goal.  

---

## 📊 Facilitation Tips
- **Timebox** → 15 minutes max.  
- **Focus** → Keep discussion on sprint goal, not problem‑solving.  
- **Visibility** → Update Jira board live during the call.  
- **Rotation** → Occasionally let team members facilitate to build ownership.  

---

| Team Member | Win | Risk | Learning | Next Target |
|-------------|-----|------|----------|-------------|
| Danish      | Completed login module | None | Learned API integration | Start testing |
| Pratiksha   | Fixed UI bug | Dependency on backend | Improved CSS knowledge | Pair with backend dev |

---

## 🌟 Key Takeaway
The Daily Scrum is not a status meeting — it’s a **commitment alignment ceremony**.  
This checklist ensures every member contributes, blockers are surfaced, and the team stays focused on sprint goals.
