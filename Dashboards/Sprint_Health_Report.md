# Sprint Health Report – Scrum Framework

## 🎯 Purpose
Provide transparency into sprint progress, predictability, and delivery health.  
This report helps stakeholders quickly assess whether the team is on track and highlights areas needing attention.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

### 1. Predictability
- **Say‑Do Ratio**: % of committed stories completed.  
- **Commitment Reliability**: Track how often sprint commitments are met.  
- **Velocity Trend**: Average story points delivered per sprint.

### 2. Delivery Health
- **Burn‑down Chart**: Remaining work vs. time.  
- **Cycle Time**: Average time from “In Progress” to “Done.”  
- **Throughput**: Number of stories completed per sprint.

### 3. Quality
- **Escaped Defects**: Bugs found after release.  
- **Defect Density**: Defects per story point delivered.  
- **UAT Readiness**: % of stories passing acceptance criteria.

### 4. Team Morale
- **Happiness Index**: Team feedback from retrospectives.  
- **Collaboration Score**: Peer survey on openness and respect.

## 🌟 Key Takeaway
A Sprint Health Report ensures **predictability, quality, and stakeholder confidence** by surfacing the most relevant Scrum metrics in a structured, executive‑ready format.
